All Featured Recipes

Margherita Pizza

Margherita pizza is a classic Italian pizza topped with simple yet flavorful ingredients: tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella cheese, basil leaves, and a drizzle of olive oil.

Pepperoni Pizza

Pepperoni pizza is a beloved classic featuring a thin or thick crust topped with tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and slices of spicy pepperoni.

Mushroom Pizza

Mushroom pizza is a delicious variation of the classic pizza, featuring a crust topped with tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and sliced mushrooms.

Carbonara

Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish known for its rich and creamy sauce. It's made with spaghetti or another long pasta, eggs, grated cheese, pancetta or guanciale, and black pepper.

Pasta

Pasta is a staple food in Italian cuisine and enjoyed worldwide for its versatility and delicious taste. It's made from durum wheat flour and water, shaped into various forms such as spaghetti, penne, fusilli, or lasagna sheets, and cooked in boiling water until tender.

Medium Rare Steak

A medium rare steak is cooked to a warm red center with a slight pink surrounding, resulting in a juicy and tender texture with rich flavors.

Baked Potato

Baked potato is a comforting dish made by baking a whole potato until it's fluffy inside and crisp outside. It's often served hot with toppings like butter, sour cream, and cheese.

Omlet

An omelet is a simple dish made by cooking whisked eggs in a pan until firm, then folding them over a variety of fillings like cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Chicken Soup

Chicken soup is a comforting dish made by simmering chicken, vegetables, and sometimes noodles or rice in a flavorful broth.

Beetroot soup

Beetroot soup is a vibrant dish made by simmering beetroot with onions, garlic, and broth, then pureeing until smooth.

Quesadillas

Quesadillas are a popular Mexican dish consisting of a tortilla filled with cheese (and often other ingredients like meat, vegetables, or beans) and then folded in half and cooked until the cheese is melted and the tortilla is crispy.

Mini Meatball Fedilini

Mini meatball fettuccine is a delightful dish featuring small meatballs served with thin pasta, often in a savory tomato or creamy sauce.

Shrimp Cocktail

Shrimp cocktail is a classic appetizer featuring chilled, cooked shrimp served with a tangy cocktail sauce made from ketchup, horseradish, lemon juice, and other seasonings.

Crostini

Crostini are bite-sized appetizers consisting of small slices of toasted bread, typically brushed with olive oil and topped with various savory or sweet toppings such as cheese, cured meats, vegetables, spreads, or fruits.

Spring Rolls

Spring rolls are a popular appetizer or snack in many Asian cuisines, including Vietnamese and Thai. They consist of thinly wrapped translucent rice paper filled with a combination of fresh vegetables, herbs, rice noodles, and often shrimp, chicken, or tofu.

Crostini

  1. Slice a baguette into 1/2 inch thick slices.
  2. Place the slices on a baking sheet and brush them lightly with olive oil.
  3. Bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for about 10-12 minutes, or until golden and crisp.
  4. Remove from the oven and let them cool slightly.
  5. Top the crostini with your favorite toppings, such as chopped tomatoes and basil, ricotta and honey, or olive tapenade.
  6. Serve immediately as an appetizer or snack.

A versatile and delicious appetizer that's perfect for any occasion.

Ingredient list

  • 1 baguette
  • Olive oil
  • Assorted toppings of your choice

Quesadillas

  1. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  2. Place a tortilla in the skillet and sprinkle shredded cheese evenly over one half of the tortilla.
  3. Optional: Add any additional fillings you like, such as cooked chicken, sautéed vegetables, or beans.
  4. Fold the other half of the tortilla over the cheese and press down gently.
  5. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, until the tortilla is golden brown and crispy and the cheese is melted.
  6. Repeat with remaining tortillas and filling.
  7. Remove quesadillas from skillet and let cool for a minute before slicing into wedges.
  8. Serve hot with salsa, guacamole, or sour cream for dipping.

A quick and easy snack or meal that's perfect for busy days.

Ingredient list

  • Flour tortillas
  • Shredded cheese (such as cheddar, Monterey Jack, or a blend)
  • Optional fillings: cooked chicken, sautéed vegetables, beans
  • Optional toppings: salsa, guacamole, sour cream

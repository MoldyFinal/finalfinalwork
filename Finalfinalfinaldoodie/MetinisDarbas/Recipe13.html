Simple Mini Meatball Fettuccine

  1. Cook fettuccine until al dente, then drain.
  2. Mix ground beef with breadcrumbs, egg, Parmesan, garlic, salt, and pepper to make mini meatballs.
  3. Brown meatballs in olive oil until cooked through.
  4. Warm marinara sauce in a separate saucepan.
  5. Toss cooked fettuccine with meatballs.
  6. Serve hot with warm marinara sauce.

A quick and tasty pasta dish with flavorful mini meatballs.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • Garnish: Chopped parsley and grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Spring Rolls

  1. Soak rice paper wrappers in warm water for about 15-20 seconds until pliable.
  2. Place a few leaves of lettuce and a few strips of your favorite vegetables (such as cucumber, carrot, and bell pepper) on the bottom third of the rice paper wrapper.
  3. Add a few cooked shrimp, chicken, or tofu strips, if desired.
  4. Roll the wrapper tightly over the filling, tucking in the sides as you go.
  5. Continue rolling until the entire wrapper is sealed.
  6. Repeat with the remaining wrappers and filling ingredients.
  7. Serve the spring rolls immediately with a dipping sauce of your choice, such as sweet chili sauce, peanut sauce, or hoisin sauce.

A fresh and flavorful appetizer or snack that's easy to make at home.

Ingredient list

  • Rice paper wrappers
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Assorted vegetables (such as cucumber, carrot, bell pepper)
  • Cooked shrimp, chicken, or tofu (optional)
  • Dipping sauce of your choice

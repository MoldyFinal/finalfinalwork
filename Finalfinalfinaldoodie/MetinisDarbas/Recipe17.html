Cheesecake

  1. Mix graham cracker crumbs with melted butter and press into a springform pan for the crust.
  2. Beat cream cheese until smooth, then add sugar, eggs, vanilla extract, and salt.
  3. Pour mixture over crust and bake until set.
  4. Cool and refrigerate before serving.
  5. Serve slices plain or with your favorite topping.

A delicious and creamy dessert that's easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 24 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

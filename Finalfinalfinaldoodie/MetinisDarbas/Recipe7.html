Medium Rare Steak

  1. Preheat grill or skillet over high heat.
  2. Season steak generously with salt and pepper.
  3. Place steak on the hot grill or skillet.
  4. Cook for 3-4 minutes on each side for medium rare, depending on thickness.
  5. Remove steak from heat and let it rest for 5 minutes.
  6. Slice against the grain and serve.

A simple recipe for a perfectly cooked medium rare steak.

Ingredient list

  • 1 steak (preferably ribeye, striploin, or tenderloin)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

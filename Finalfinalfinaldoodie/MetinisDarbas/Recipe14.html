Simple Shrimp Cocktail

  1. Cook shrimp in boiling salted water for 2-3 minutes until pink.
  2. Chill shrimp in ice water, then peel and devein.
  3. Mix ketchup, horseradish, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, and hot sauce for cocktail sauce.
  4. Arrange shrimp on a platter with cocktail sauce on the side.
  5. Garnish with lemon wedges and parsley if desired.
  6. Serve chilled and enjoy!

An easy-to-make classic appetizer that's always a hit.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • Salt
  • For cocktail sauce:
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons prepared horseradish
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Hot sauce to taste
  • Garnish: Lemon wedges, fresh parsley

Baked Potatoes

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Scrub potatoes clean under running water and pat dry.
  3. Poke each potato several times with a fork or knife to allow steam to escape during baking.
  4. Lightly coat each potato with olive oil and sprinkle with salt.
  5. Place potatoes directly on the oven rack or on a baking sheet lined with aluminum foil.
  6. Bake for 45-60 minutes, or until potatoes are tender when pierced with a fork.
  7. Remove from oven and let cool for a few minutes before serving.

A classic side dish that's easy to make and pairs well with a variety of main courses.

Ingredient list

  • 4 medium-sized potatoes (russet or any variety suitable for baking)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

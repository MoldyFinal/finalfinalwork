Simple Macarons

  1. Mix almond flour and powdered sugar together.
  2. Beat egg whites until stiff, then gently fold in granulated sugar.
  3. Combine the almond mixture with the egg whites to form a batter.
  4. Pipe small rounds onto a baking sheet and let sit for 30 minutes.
  5. Bake until set and cool completely.
  6. Fill with your favorite filling and sandwich together.
  7. Store in the refrigerator before serving.

A delightful French treat that's easier to make than you think!

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups almond flour
  • 1 3/4 cups powdered sugar
  • 3 large egg whites, room temperature
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • Food coloring (optional)
  • Filling of your choice (e.g., buttercream, ganache)

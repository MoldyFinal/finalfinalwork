Simple Chicken Soup

  1. Saute onions, carrots, and celery in olive oil until softened.
  2. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Pour in chicken broth and bring to a simmer.
  4. Add diced cooked chicken and season with salt, pepper, and herbs.
  5. Simmer for 20-30 minutes.
  6. Add cooked noodles or rice if desired.
  7. Adjust seasoning and serve hot.

A comforting and easy-to-make chicken soup perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups diced cooked chicken
  • Salt, pepper, herbs (optional: noodles or rice)

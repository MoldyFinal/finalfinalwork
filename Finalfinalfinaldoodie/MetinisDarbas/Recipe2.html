Pepperoni Pizza

  1. Preheat your oven to its highest temperature.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread pizza sauce over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Arrange pepperoni slices on top.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden brown, then slice and serve your pepperoni pizza!

Ingredient list

      Pizza dough
      Pizza sauce
      Shredded mozzarella cheese.
      Pepperoni slices.
      Olive oil.
      Optional: dried oregano, crushed red pepper flakes.

Bologna lasagna

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Cook noodles according to package instructions. Drain
  3. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil. Add ground beef/sausage and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, marinara, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheeses.
  6. Layer sauce, noodles, cheese mixture in baking dish, ending with sauce on top.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for 10-15 minutes until golden.
  8. Let cool, then slice and serve your Bologna lasagna!

Ingredient list

      9 lasagna noodles
      1 lb (450g) ground beef or Italian sausage
      1 onion, finely chopped
      2 cloves garlic, minced
      1 can (28 oz / 800g) crushed tomatoes
      2 cups (480ml) marinara sauce
      1 cup (240ml) shredded mozzarella cheese
      1/2 cup (120ml) grated Parmesan cheese
      1 teaspoon each dried basil and oregano
      Salt and pepper, to taste
      Olive oil, for cooking

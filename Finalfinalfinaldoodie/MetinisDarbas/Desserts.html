Cheesecake

Cheesecake is a decadent dessert made with a rich, creamy filling, typically consisting of cream cheese, sugar, and eggs, poured over a crust, often made from crushed graham crackers or cookies.

Crème Brûlée

Crème brûlée is a classic French dessert consisting of a rich custard base made from cream, egg yolks, sugar, and vanilla, which is then baked until set.

Macarons

Macarons are delicate and colorful French pastries made from almond flour, egg whites, and sugar, sandwiched together with a flavored filling such as ganache, buttercream, or fruit preserves.

Chocolate Chip Cookie

Chocolate chip cookies are classic American treats loved for their irresistible combination of buttery, chewy dough and sweet, melty chocolate chips.

Red Velvet Cupcakes

Red velvet cupcakes are moist and decadent treats with a distinctive deep red color and a hint of cocoa flavor. They're made with a combination of flour, cocoa powder, buttermilk, sugar, eggs, and a touch of vinegar and baking soda to achieve their characteristic texture and color.

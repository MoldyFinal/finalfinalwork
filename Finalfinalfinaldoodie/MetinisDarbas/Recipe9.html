Basic Omelet

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and beat them with a fork or whisk until well mixed.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add a small amount of butter or oil.
  3. Pour the beaten eggs into the skillet.
  4. Let the eggs cook undisturbed for about 1 minute, until the edges start to set.
  5. Using a spatula, gently push the cooked edges towards the center, tilting the skillet to let the uncooked egg mixture flow to the edges.
  6. Continue cooking for another 2-3 minutes, until the omelet is mostly set but still slightly runny on top.
  7. Add your desired filling (e.g., cheese, vegetables, ham) to one half of the omelet.
  8. Using the spatula, fold the other half of the omelet over the filling.
  9. Cook for another 1-2 minutes, until the cheese melts and the omelet is cooked through.
  10. Slide the omelet onto a plate and serve immediately.

A quick and easy breakfast or brunch option that you can customize with your favorite fillings.

Ingredient list

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Butter or oil for cooking
  • Filling of your choice (e.g., cheese, vegetables, ham)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

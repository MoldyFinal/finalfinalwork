Beetroot Soup

  1. Saute onions, beetroots, carrots, and potatoes in olive oil.
  2. Add broth and simmer until vegetables are tender.
  3. Blend the soup until smooth.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and vinegar or lemon juice.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with sour cream or herbs if desired.

An easy and delicious soup with the vibrant flavor of beetroots.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium-sized beetroots, peeled and chopped
  • 2 carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 6 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • Salt, pepper, vinegar or lemon juice
  • Garnish: sour cream, fresh herbs (optional)
